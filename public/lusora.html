FAQ

Know Before You Glow

From what to wear to how early to arrive, we’ve gathered the most common questions guests ask before their first visit

What should I wear to my appointment?

You’re welcome to come in whatever feels comfortable. For most treatments, we provide a robe, slippers, and a private space to change. During your service, your therapist will ensure you're properly draped at all times to maintain your privacy and comfort.

How early should I arrive to my appointment?

We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes early to check in, enjoy a calming tea, and begin to unwind before your treatment begins. Arriving late may shorten your session so we can stay on schedule for the next guest.

Can I choose my therapist?

Yes. If you have a preferred therapist or would feel more comfortable with a therapist of a specific gender, just let us know when booking. We do our best to accommodate all requests.

What if I have a medical condition?

Please inform us of any allergies, sensitivities, or medical conditions when you book your appointment. This helps us tailor your treatment safely and effectively. If you're unsure about any treatment, our staff is happy to guide you.

Should I tip my therapist?

Gratuities are always appreciated but never expected. If you'd like to leave a tip, you can do so in cash or add it to your payment at checkout. A typical gratuity ranges from 15–20% of the service price.
